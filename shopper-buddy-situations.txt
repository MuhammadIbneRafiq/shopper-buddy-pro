
SHOPPER BUDDY SITUATIONAL KNOWLEDGE BASE

SITUATION: checkout_public_place
Context: User is at checkout surrounded by people. Cannot speak aloud.
Constraint: No voice input available. Use button mode only.
Action: Silently read total, proceed to payment without asking.
Voice prompt: null

SITUATION: low_balance_warning  
Context: Account balance is less than basket total.
Constraint: Payment will fail.
Action: Warn user before attempting payment.
Voice prompt: "Warning: your balance may not cover this shop. Your balance is X euros, basket is Y euros."

SITUATION: product_unrecognized
Context: Camera scan returned no match or price is zero.
Constraint: Cannot add item with unknown price.
Action: Query knowledge base for product, ask user to confirm price.
Voice prompt: "I couldn't identify the price. Can you confirm this product?"

SITUATION: basket_empty_checkout
Context: User tries to checkout with empty basket.
Action: Inform user basket is empty.
Voice prompt: "Your basket is empty. Scan a product first."

SITUATION: first_time_user
Context: App just opened, user has not chosen input mode.
Action: Speak welcome and listen for mode choice.
Voice prompt: "Welcome to Shopper Buddy. Say button or voice to choose your mode."

SITUATION: scanning_in_progress
Context: Camera is active and scanning.
Constraint: Do not interrupt with questions.
Action: Wait for scan result silently.

SITUATION: payment_processing
Context: BUNQ payment is being processed.
Constraint: Do not allow new scans or basket changes.
Action: Wait silently, speak result when done.

SITUATION: product_added_confirmation
Context: Product was successfully added to basket.
Action: Briefly confirm and return to scan mode.
Voice prompt: "Added. Ready to scan next product."

SITUATION: allergen_detected
Context: Scanned product contains allergens matching user profile.
Action: Warn before adding to basket.
Voice prompt: "Warning: this product contains X. Do you still want to add it?"
